University of Rochester, Bangladesh Students Association

Welcome to the University of Rochester Bangladesh Student Association (URBSA) website! At URBSA, we are dedicated to preserving and celebrating Bangladeshi culture right at the heart of the University of Rochester community. Our mission is to foster a sense of belonging among Bangladeshi students by organizing vibrant cultural events and providing a platform for connection and support. Join us as we strive to create a welcoming environment where every Bangladeshi student feels at home.
